# Job Profiles

## office_computer

Typical risks: prolonged sitting, neck flexion, wrist extension, shoulder tension, eye strain.

Reminder mix: eyes, neck/shoulders, wrists/hands, posture reset, walking.

## call_center

Typical risks: headset use, static sitting, keyboard/mouse repetition, limited break autonomy.

Reminder mix: shoulders, neck, jaw relaxation, eye break, breathing, headset side-switch reminder.

## control_room

Typical risks: long screen monitoring, high attention load, long shifts, low movement.

Reminder mix: eye breaks, posture reset, standing/walking, breathing, hydration.

## driver

Typical risks: prolonged sitting, vibration, limited movement windows, hip/back stiffness.

Reminder mix: scheduled stop stretches, shoulder reset, ankle pumps when safe and parked.

## warehouse_terminal

Typical risks: standing, walking, lifting, terminal input, uneven posture.

Reminder mix: foot/ankle reset, back-neutral reminder, shoulder/wrist reset, hydration.
