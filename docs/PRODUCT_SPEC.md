# Product Specification

## Product name

Working name: ErgoMove

## Problem

Many office and computer-based workers maintain static, low-movement postures for long periods. Generic timers exist, but they usually do not adapt to job type, language, or ergonomic context.

## Target users

- Office and administrative staff
- Computer-heavy workers and developers
- Call-center operators
- Security/control-room operators
- Drivers and dispatch staff
- Warehouse staff who alternate between standing and computer terminals

## Differentiation

1. Job-profile based reminders rather than a single fixed timer.
2. Persian + English content from the start.
3. Structured content database with source notes.
4. Windows-first but cross-platform architecture.
5. Enterprise-ready future: admin policy, team statistics, custom company reminders.

## MVP features

- Language selector: Persian / English
- Job profile selector
- Configurable workday window
- Micro-breaks: eye, posture, wrists/hands
- Rest breaks: stand, walk, hydrate, longer stretch
- Snooze / skip with reason
- Local-only settings
- Simple history: completed, skipped, snoozed

## Non-goals for MVP

- Medical diagnosis
- Pain treatment plans
- AI posture detection through camera
- Cloud accounts
- Employer surveillance

## Future modules

- Android app
- macOS app
- Admin dashboard for companies
- Custom reminder packs
- Offline educational cards
- Optional wearable integration
- Calendar-aware quiet mode
- Privacy-first analytics
